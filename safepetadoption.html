Safety and Animal Welfare is Our Priority

Here at Benji, we're all about creating a pet adoption journey that's safe and fulfilling. We've got some key guidelines for our users to follow, making sure everyone has a great experience.

Get Verified

User verification is our way of making sure everyone in our Benji community is real, honest, and totally dedicated to giving pets the loving homes they deserve.

This step helps us keep out scams and any funny business o ensure that all the information about the adorable pets is spot-on and current.

With Benji, you can relax and feel good knowing that your new furry friend is coming from a place you can trust, and that their previous owner really cares about finding them a forever home full of love.

Take a Look at Our Pet Adoption Agreement

We provide a free pet adoption agreement at Benji, helping everyone have a smooth and secure journey to adopting a furry friend.

This agreement lays out all the need-to-knows for both the pet's current owner and the lucky person adopting. It covers everything from who's responsible for what, to the nitty-gritty details of the adoption.

For those looking to adopt, it includes important info like the pet's medical history and a health guarantee for a certain period after you bring your new buddy home.

And for the pet's current owner, it details what's expected from the adopter in terms of taking care of their new furry family member's health and happiness.

Explore Public Profiles

Public profiles on Benji play a big role in making pet adoption both safer and more friendly.

Pet owners on Benji can share their reasons for rehoming their pet, and those looking to adopt can learn more about the owners. This really helps everyone feel more connected and secure throughout the adoption process.

Check Out Our Guidelines

We've put together some handy tips to help you find your perfect furry friend:

Do a bit of homework: Before you adopt, look into the type of pet you're considering. Think about their breed, size, and what they need to be happy and healthy in your home.

Meet the pet: Before making a final decision, it's super important to meet your potential new pet in person. This way, you can make sure you both click!

Keep an eye out for warning signs: Watch out for anyone asking for a lot of money upfront or who doesn’t want to meet you and the pet together.

Go for the gold (or verified badge!): When looking for a pet on our platform, try to choose pet owners who have that verified user badge.

Double-check their background: Do a little detective work on the pet owner's history. Ask for information from other users to make sure they're the real deal.

Be smart about the adoption fee: If the price seems unbelievably low, it might be a scam. Remember, if it sounds too good to be true, it probably is!
