Safety and Animal Welfare is Our Priority

At Benji, we focus on providing a pet adoption experience that’s both safe and rewarding. We have a set of principles that we ask all our users to follow, ensuring a trusted process for everyone.

Get Verified

User verification helps us ensure that our community is made up of genuine, trustworthy individuals who are committed to finding pets loving homes.

This helps reduce any potential scams or fraudulent activity on our platform, and ensures that the information that pet owners share about their pets is accurate and up-to-date.

With Benji, you can have peace of mind knowing that the pet you're adopting is coming from a trustworthy source and that the pet's owner is committed to finding their pet a loving, permanent home.

Use Our Pet Adoption Agreement

We offer a pet adoption agreement that helps make the adoption journey safer for everyone involved.

Our pet adoption agreement outlines the terms and conditions of the adoption, including the responsibilities of both the pet owner and the pet adopter.

For the prospective adopter, the agreement captures things like disclosures about the pet's medical history. It's also helpful to get a guarantee of the pet's health within a specified amount of time after the adoption.

For the current owner of the pet, the agreement also describes the responsibilities of the adopter including commitment to the health and wellbeing of their new family member.

Check Public Profiles

Public profiles play an important role in making pet adoption safer.

It helps to build trust and transparency between pet owners and pet adopters. Pet owners can share information about their motivations for rehoming their pets. And pet adopters can learn more about the pet owners.

Follow Our Guidelines

We've developed a set of general pet adoption guidelines that we encourage all pet adopters to follow:

Do your research: Before adopting a pet, it's important to research the breed, size, and care requirements to ensure that the pet is a good fit for your lifestyle.

Meet the pet: Before making a final decision, it's important to meet the pet in person to ensure that you have a strong connection.

Beware of red flags: Be wary of pet owners who ask for large sums of money upfront, or who are reluctant to meet you in person.

Adopt from verified users: Look for pet owners with a verified user badge when searching for a pet to adopt on our platform.

Check the credentials: Make sure to verify the credentials of the pet owner before adopting a pet. Look for reviews, ratings, and feedback from other users to ensure that they are legitimate.

Check the adoption fee: Be wary of prices that seem too good to be true, as scammers often offer puppies at lower-than-market prices to attract potential buyers.
